Audio and Music Production

Here are a few of my audio projects that I have been working on throught my time at ISU.

This is a Foley Narrative I created with my group. We used everyday objects such as jackets and tissue paper to replicate the sounds of someone walking into a classroom, sitting down, pulling out their computer, and writing an essay about the camping trip they went on that weekend.

This is a Loop Based Composition I created with just the sounds of one of my group members beatboxing, my voice, and someone playing piano.
